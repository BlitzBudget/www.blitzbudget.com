Get Started

Once you've downloaded the Amazon Alexa app, you can enable the BlitzBudget skill in a snap.

1

Enable the skill

Open the Alexa app or go to Amazon.com. Select “Skills” and search for BlitzBudget. Select “Enable”.

2

Link your account

Enter your BlitzBudget username and password. Optionally, create a personal key.

3

Talk to Alexa

Now you can talk to Alexa about your BlitzBudget account.
